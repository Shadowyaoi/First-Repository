Welcome to our little slice of home!

Welcome to what we are truely passionate about, good food and great company! We love that you decided to give our place a try, we pride ourselfs on fresh ingredients and stellar service. Once you step into our place, you become family, and like any family we want you to be well fed and well taken care of. Your happiness is our job and your satisfaction is our goal. Our Italian style food is here to feed your soul, so please, take your time, enjoy yourself, and truly feel what it means to be a part of our family!

We specialize in pizza but we also have lovely pasta, soups, salads and bread, all handmade by our lovely family, and with handmade sauces, it can't be beat! Our specialty is Margarita pizza with handmade mozzarella, fresh basil, and beautiful tomatoes, all neatly presented on our handmade sourdough crust, our special recipe thanks to Tammy. She makes her own sourdough, so all our breads, crusts, and doughs are made from her special strain. If you have any accommodations (allergies, vegan, vegetarian, etc.) please do not hesitate to let your server know, we will be more then happy to accommodate. Have a lovely day and enjoy!
